Privacy Policy

Always Reading  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Always Reading is a personal ebook reader for Android. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data we collect

None. Always Reading does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no network requests of any kind.

Data stored on your device

The app stores the following locally on your device, solely to make the app work:

  • The ebook files you import
  • Your reading position and progress
  • Your in-app preferences (font size, etc.)

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-party services

Always Reading does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or crash-reporting tools.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
